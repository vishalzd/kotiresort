Cement Company Scope 3 Offset Program

Client Profile: Mid-sized cement manufacturer | Annual production: 2.5 million tonnes | ESG Target: Net Zero Scope 3 by 2030

Challenge

Company needed to offset 80,000 tCO2e annually from indirect emissions but had no carbon procurement experience or buyer network.

Our Solution

  • Conducted carbon footprint analysis and offset needs assessment
  • Designed diversified portfolio: 60% renewable energy + 40% energy efficiency credits
  • Sourced 80,000 VCUs annually from 5 projects (Verra VCS registry)
  • Negotiated multi-year offtake agreement with 15% discount to spot market
  • Provided ESG reporting support for CDP and GRI disclosures

Outcome

15% Below Market Cost Savings Achieved
3-Year Contract Supply Security

Result: Achieved Net Zero Scope 3 target 2 years ahead of schedule at 15% below market pricing. Client secured stable 3-year supply with locked-in pricing.
